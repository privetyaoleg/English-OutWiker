!!!! Lend vs. Borrow


* ''''LEND'''' - ''give'' something (for a short period of time) TO someone

* ''''BORROW'''' - ''take'' something (for a short period of time) FROM someone
(:source lang="":)
Michael asked John, "Can I borrow your hat?"
John told Michael, "Yes, you can borrow it from me, I will lend you"
Michael borrowed a hat from John. John lent his hat to Michael.
(:sourceend:)
