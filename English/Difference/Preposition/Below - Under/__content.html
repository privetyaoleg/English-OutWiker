Below vs. Under


BELOW - ниже
  1. lower than
  2. not directly under
  3. measurements
  • It's below the cabinet
  • We stopped 100 meters below the top of the mountain
  • It's 5 below 0o
  • We are at 150 feet below sea level
UNDER - под
  1. lower than
  2. covered
  3. less than
  • It's under the sink
  • The cat is under the bed
  • He's under 18
  • There were under 5 people at the office today
